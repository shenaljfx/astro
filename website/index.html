FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?
Grahachara gives you access to all features — Rahu Kalaya, Nakath, Panchanga, birth charts, 10 daily chat questions, push notifications, dream interpretation and more — for just LKR 8/day (about Rs. 240/month). Premium add-ons like the full Life Report (LKR 350) and Porondam matching (LKR 50) are one-time pay-per-use. No hidden fees, cancel anytime.
Which Ayanamsha does it use?
We use the Lahiri Ayanamsha — the traditional standard used across Sri Lanka and India. All calculations are sidereal (not tropical/Western), matching what your family astrologer uses.
How accurate are the calculations?
Our engine uses the Meeus astronomical algorithms and ephemeris data for planetary positions. We verified Lagna accuracy against multiple known birth charts with exact matches. The Panchanga is calculated for your exact GPS coordinates.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. Your birth data is stored securely and never sold or shared. We don't run ads or sell user profiles. Your cosmic data stays between you and the stars. 🌟
What languages are supported?
The entire app — every screen, button, and response — is available in සිංහල (Sinhala) and English. You choose your language during onboarding and can switch anytime from settings.
How does the Grahachara astrologer chat work?
When you ask a question, Grahachara reads your birth chart (planet positions, house placements, current Dasha period) and combines it with real-time planetary transits to generate a deeply personal, context-aware response — not generic horoscopes.
