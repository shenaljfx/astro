FAQ

Questions? Answered.

What is Vedic astrology?

A 5,000-year-old system from ancient India that tracks actual star positions using the sidereal zodiac — astronomically precise, unlike Western astrology which has drifted ~24° from the real sky.

How accurate are the readings?

Grahachara processes your complete birth chart — all planetary positions, house placements, aspects, Dasha periods, and live transits — simultaneously. Hundreds of variables weighed to deliver deeply personal insights.

Is my birth data secure and private?

Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell or share your information. Delete your account and all data at any time from within the app.

Do I need my exact birth time?

For maximum accuracy, yes — your Ascendant changes every 2 hours. Grahachara still delivers valuable insights with approximate times, but precision improves predictions.

What do I get with Grahachara?

Complete birth chart analysis, daily personalized predictions, compatibility matching, a 22-chapter life report with 25-year timeline, dream interpretation, and your own personal astrologer — all in one app.

Is this available in my country?

Grahachara is available worldwide on Google Play. Your readings are personalized to your exact birth location and time, regardless of where you are now.
