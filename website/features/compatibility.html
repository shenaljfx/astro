Vedic Compatibility Matching

Discover your cosmic connection. The ancient 36-point Ashtakoot system reveals if your stars align — for love, marriage, or partnership.

How Vedic Compatibility Works

Vedic compatibility (Kundali Milan) compares two birth charts across 8 dimensions called Koota. Each dimension tests a different aspect of the relationship — from physical attraction to spiritual harmony. Together they form the Ashtakoot system, scoring up to 36 points.

A score above 18 indicates a favorable match. Above 24 is considered excellent. Below 18 may indicate challenges requiring deeper analysis of planetary periods (Dasha) and remedial measures.

The 8 Dimensions of Matching

  • Varna (1 point) — Spiritual compatibility and ego levels
  • Vashya (2 points) — Mutual attraction and influence
  • Tara (3 points) — Health and well-being of the couple
  • Yoni (4 points) — Physical and sexual compatibility
  • Graha Maitri (5 points) — Mental wavelength and friendship
  • Gana (6 points) — Temperament alignment (Deva/Manushya/Rakshasa)
  • Bhakoot (7 points) — Emotional connection and prosperity
  • Nadi (8 points) — Genetic and health compatibility

Mangal Dosha Analysis

Mars placement in certain houses (1st, 4th, 7th, 8th, 12th) creates Mangal Dosha — a factor that can affect marital harmony. Grahachara automatically detects this in both charts and identifies if Dosha cancellation conditions apply, saving you from unnecessary worry.

We also check for Nadi Dosha (genetic incompatibility) and Bhakoot Dosha (financial strain) — with explanations of exceptions and remedies.

Beyond the Score

Numbers tell only part of the story. Grahachara goes deeper:

  • Nakshatra Synastry — How your lunar mansions interact
  • 7th Lord Analysis — The quality of partnership energy in each chart
  • Venus & Jupiter positions — Love language and values alignment
  • Dasha Overlap — Whether your planetary periods support the relationship timeline
