25-Year Life Report

Your next quarter century, mapped through the lens of Vimshottari Dasha. Every planetary period decoded into actionable life chapters.

What Is a Vedic Life Report?

In Vedic astrology, your life unfolds in planetary periods called Dashas. Each Dasha lasts years, ruled by a specific planet that colors everything — your opportunities, challenges, relationships, and growth.

Grahachara's Life Report maps your next 25 years of Dasha transitions, explaining what each period brings and how to navigate it. Think of it as a cosmic roadmap — not predicting the exact turns, but showing you the terrain ahead.

How the Vimshottari System Works

The Vimshottari Dasha system divides life into a 120-year cycle across 9 planets:

  • Sun (6 years) — Authority, career peaks, father figures
  • Moon (10 years) — Emotional depth, mother, public life
  • Mars (7 years) — Drive, property, siblings, courage
  • Rahu (18 years) — Ambition, unconventional paths, obsession
  • Jupiter (16 years) — Wisdom, expansion, children, spirituality
  • Saturn (19 years) — Discipline, delays that build, karmic lessons
  • Mercury (17 years) — Communication, business, learning
  • Ketu (7 years) — Detachment, spiritual evolution, past-life themes
  • Venus (20 years) — Love, luxury, creativity, partnerships

Your report pinpoints exactly where you are in this cycle and what's coming next.

What Your Report Covers

Each chapter in your life report includes:

  • Period Overview — The ruling planet's energy and how it interacts with your chart
  • Career & Finance — Growth periods, career shifts, financial windfalls or cautions
  • Relationships — Marriage timing, partnership dynamics, family events
  • Health — Vulnerable areas and optimal wellness strategies for each period
  • Key Dates — Sub-period transitions that trigger significant life events
  • Remedial Guidance — Mantras, gemstones, and practices to optimize each period

Sample Insight

"Your Jupiter Mahadasha begins mid-2026, activating your 9th house. Expect opportunities in higher education, international connections, and a deepening spiritual practice. This 16-year period favors teaching, publishing, and mentorship roles."

Every insight is generated from your specific planetary placements — not generic planet descriptions.
