Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026 · Effective: March 25, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Wenthura Solutions Pvt Ltd ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website grahachara.com. By continuing to browse our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described below.

1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience. Cookies may be "session" cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent" cookies (remain until they expire or you delete them).

2 Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They cannot be disabled.

  • Language Preference: Stores your selected language (English or Sinhala).
  • Session Management: Maintains your browsing session.

2.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information.

  • Page Views: Which pages are visited and how often.
  • Traffic Sources: How visitors find our website.
  • Device & Browser Info: Aggregated, anonymous technical data.

Note: We use privacy-focused analytics. All data is aggregated and anonymized — we cannot identify individual users from analytics data.

2.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization:

  • Theme Preference: Remembers your visual preferences.
  • Recently Viewed: Stores recently viewed features for quicker access.

3 Third-Party Cookies

We minimize third-party cookies on our website. The only third-party services that may set cookies are:

  • Google Fonts: For loading our website fonts (Inter, Space Grotesk, Noto Sans Sinhala). Google may collect anonymous usage data.

We do not use advertising cookies, social media tracking pixels, or remarketing services on our website.

4 Mobile App

Our mobile application does not use browser cookies. The app may use similar technologies (local storage, device identifiers) for functionality purposes as described in our Privacy Policy.

5 Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Here's how:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, Search and Services → Cookies

Please note that disabling essential cookies may impact the functionality of our website.

6 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
