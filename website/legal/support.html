🔧 Troubleshooting

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Ensure you're running the latest version of Grahachara.
  • Restart your device and reopen the app.
  • Clear the app cache (Settings → Storage → Clear Cache).
  • If the issue persists, reinstall the app.

Login Issues

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Use the "Forgot Password" option to reset your credentials.
  • If you signed up with Google/Apple, use the same sign-in method.

Incorrect Astrological Data

  • Verify your birth date, time, and location in Settings → Profile.
  • Even a few minutes' difference in birth time can affect chart calculations.
  • Ensure your device's timezone is set correctly.
